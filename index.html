Stem Cell Donation Awareness

What is Stem Cell Donation?

Stem cell donation is a life-saving procedure where healthy individuals donate their stem cells to help patients with blood cancers and other life-threatening diseases.

Donation Statistics

India

Less than 0.04% of the population is registered as a stem cell donor.

Global

Over 37 million registered stem cell donors worldwide.

How Can You Donate?

To donate stem cells, register with an authorized organization like DATRI or DKMS-BMST. If you match with a patient, a simple and painless procedure will be followed to collect your stem cells.
